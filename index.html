About GBRP

Great Britain Roleplays (GBRP) is a UK-based ERLC roleplay community that focuses on realistic, British-style emergency response gameplay in Emergency Response: Liberty County (Roblox).

Our goal is to create a friendly, professional, and immersive experience for all players.


As staff, you represent the foundation of that experience â€” from moderating the server and managing roleplays to supporting members and enforcing rules.


Staff Responsibilities

All staff members are expected to:

  • Uphold the GBRP rules and guidelines at all times.
  • Remain professional and unbiased in all decisions.
  • Assist players respectfully, no matter their experience level.
  • Keep the community active, organized, and free from toxicity.
  • Report issues or major incidents to higher staff immediately.

Staff Structure

Leadership Team

  • Oversees the entire server, manages high-level decisions, partnerships, and development.

Senior Staff

  • Manages department coordination, training, and handles advanced moderation issues.

Moderation Team

  • Ensures rule enforcement in both Discord and in-game sessions.
  • Handles player disputes, reports, and assists during RP sessions.

Support Team

  • Welcomes new members, answers basic questions, and monitors chat activity.

Staff Conduct Rules

These apply specifically to staff members:

  1. Confidentiality: Do not leak internal information or screenshots.
  2. Professionalism: Always act maturely when representing GBRP.
  3. No Abuse of Power: Never use permissions or commands unfairly.
  4. Teamwork: Communicate clearly and respectfully with other staff.
  5. Activity: Stay active in Discord and sessions â€” inactivity may lead to demotion.
  6. Decision-Making: Always base actions on the rules, not personal feelings.
